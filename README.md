Goal #1 – Contribute to Modernization and Cloud Migration

Actively contribute to the modernization of legacy systems by supporting architectural improvements and assisting in the migration of existing on-premise products to cloud-based platforms, helping improve scalability, reliability, and developer productivity.

Goal #2 – Support Workflow Migration to Cloud Infrastructure

Collaborate with the team to transition existing operational workflows from on-premise environments to cloud infrastructure, ensuring smooth migration, improved automation, and alignment with the organization’s cloud strategy.

Goal #3 – Strengthen Technical Knowledge and Team Collaboration

Continuously expand knowledge of the team’s technology stack and engineering practices by actively participating in technical discussions, hackathons, and internal learning initiatives, while also sharing knowledge through technical sessions and collaborative problem solving.

Goal #4 – Explore and Apply AI-Driven Solutions

Develop foundational expertise in Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies, and explore opportunities to incorporate AI-driven solutions into projects to improve efficiency, innovation, and product capabilities.
